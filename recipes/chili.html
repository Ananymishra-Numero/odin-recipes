Chili Paneer

Description

Chili Paneer is a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer made with crispy paneer cubes tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce with bell peppers and onions. It's flavorful, quick to prepare, and perfect as a starter or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix paneer with cornflour, flour, salt, and a little water to coat.
  2. Deep fry or shallow fry the paneer until golden and crispy. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp oil and saut√© garlic, onions, and bell peppers.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, and ketchup. Stir well.
  5. Add fried paneer cubes and toss to coat evenly with sauce.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot.
